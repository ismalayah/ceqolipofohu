Bryana Salaz is an American singer and actress, who was born on 25th August 1997. She was raised in a military family by her parents, most notably her father, Colonel Edward Salaz.

Between her freshman and senior years, Salaz went to four high schools. She learned to adapt from an early age since she moved from one place to another every 3-4 years.

The singer-cum-actress is best known for being a part of Gwen Stefani’s team in The Voice season 7. Additionally, she gained significant stardom following her performance as Kaylie in Netflix’s comedy web series Team Kaylie (2019).

The role of Kaylie in the Netflix series 'Team Kaylie' (2019)

Team Kaylie is a comedy web series starring Bryana Salaz as Kaylie Konrad, Rosa Blasi as Amber, and Alison Fernandez as Jackie.

The story revolves around Bryana’s character Kaylie Konrad who is a 19-year-old billionaire. Kaylie is sentenced to community service following a car accident. She then becomes the leader of the Wilderness Club at an inner-city middle school.

Bryana Salaz Career and Net Worth

Bryana Salaz's net worth is estimated to be $1.5 million.
Source: Bryana Salaz Instagram (@bryanasalaz)

Bryana Salaz’s initial acting credit was the role of Anita in the unaired pilot of Urban Cowboy (2015). A year later, she had a recurring role as Daisy in a comedy series Best Friends Whenever (2016). The same year she featured as Becky in the comedy film Bizaardvark (2016).

Salaz’s major breakthrough came in 2019 when she landed the main role of Anga in The Lion Guard for 20 episodes. The same year, Bryana featured for a single episode titled Gone Baby Gone in an animated series Star vs. the Forces of Evil (2019) as Teen Meteora. This was followed by a recurring role as Logan for seven episodes in a comedy series Malibu Rescue (2019).

Besides her acting career, Bryana is a well-renowned singer who grew an interest in music at a young age following encouragement from her mother. In 2014, she competed in The Voice Season 7, where she reached the playoffs prior to getting eliminated, subsequently missing out on top 12.

Watch: Canal Sony | The Voice T7 - Knockouts Pt 1 - Bryana Salaz "Heart Attack"

Salaz joined girl-band Sweet Suspense replacing Celine Polenghi on 11th June 2015. She later left the group to pursue a career in acting. In 2016, the singer subsequently joined the season 2 cast of the comedy series Best Friends Whenever.

The actress formed a new girl band called SNRG upon reuniting with Celine and former Sweet Suspense member Summer Reign; however, she soon split from the group in May 2016.
