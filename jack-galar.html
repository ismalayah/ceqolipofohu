Jack[1] (Japanese: ポケモンセンターの職員[2] Pokémon Center Staff) is a character in Pokémon Sword and Shield who can be found behind the left counter in every Pokémon Center in Galar. He offers the services of the Name Rater, Memory Girl, Move Reminder, and Move Deleter from previous games, being able to change a Pokémon's nickname, check a Pokémon's memories (after the player has obtained three Badges), have a Pokémon remember a move, and have a Pokémon forget a move. All of Jack's services are free of charge.

His name is derived from the term 'jack-of-all-trades', referring to the multiple different services he can provide. He shares his model with the Café Master Trainer class. He is always accompanied by an Indeedee, the gender of which varies depending on the specific Pokémon Center.
