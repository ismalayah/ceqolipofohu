Who is Joe Klecko:

Joe Klecko is a famous Football Player. He was born on October 15, 1953 and his birthplace is Pennsylvania. Joe is also well known as, Retired defensive lineman who made four Pro Bowls and was named AFC Defensive Player of the Year during his illustrious career. Following his retirement, he was inducted into the New York Jets Ring of Honor and saw his #73 retired by the Jets organization.

Joe is originated from United States. He and “Broadway” Joe Namath both had their jerseys retired by the New York Jets.
