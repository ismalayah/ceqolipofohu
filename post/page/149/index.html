published on April 26, 2024
From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia. Jack[1] (Japanese: ポケモンセンターの職員[2] Pokémon Center Staff) is a character in Pokémon Sword and Shield who can be found behind the left counter in every Pokémon Center in Galar. He offers the services of the Name Rater, Memory Girl, Move Reminder, and Move Deleter from previous games, being able to change a Pokémon's nickname, check a Pokémon's memories (after the player has obtained three Badges), have a Pokémon remember a move, and have a Pokémon forget a move.
